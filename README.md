# Telco Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project presents an end-to-end Business Intelligence dashboard designed to analyze customer churn behavior, customer segmentation, and revenue performance in a telecommunications company. The dashboard was built using Power BI, SQL, DAX, and Power Query to transform raw customer data into actionable business insights and strategic recommendations.

---

## Business Objectives
The primary objectives of this project are:

- Analyze customer churn patterns
- Identify key churn drivers
- Evaluate customer loyalty and retention
- Monitor revenue performance
- Provide strategic business recommendations
- Support data-driven decision making

---

## Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- MySQL
- SQL
- DAX
- Power Query
- Data Modeling
- Business Intelligence

---

## Dataset
Dataset Source:
Telco Customer Churn Dataset from Kaggle

The dataset contains customer demographic information, subscription details, payment methods, internet services, monthly charges, tenure, and churn status.

---

## Data Preparation
The data preparation process included:

- Data cleaning using Power Query
- Handling missing and inconsistent values
- Creating calculated columns and DAX measures
- Building star schema data modeling
- Creating KPI metrics for business analysis

---

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Overview Dashboard
Provides high-level KPIs and overall business performance insights, including:
- Total customers
- Churn rate
- Total revenue
- Revenue loss
- Customer segmentation overview

---

### 2. Customer Analytics Dashboard
Focuses on customer demographics and behavioral analysis:
- Customer loyalty analysis
- Revenue contribution by customer segment
- Customer demographic insights
- Monthly charge distribution

---

### 3. Churn Analysis Dashboard
Analyzes customer churn behavior and retention risks:
- Churn by contract type
- Churn by payment method
- Churn by internet service
- Tenure group churn analysis
- Churn risk segmentation

---

### 4. Revenue Intelligence Dashboard
Provides financial and revenue-focused insights:
- Revenue by customer segment
- Revenue by contract type
- Revenue distribution analysis
- Revenue loss due to churn
- Customer value analysis

---

### 5. Business Recommendation Dashboard
Presents strategic business insights and recommendations:
- Key business findings
- Retention strategy recommendations
- Revenue optimization opportunities
- Customer loyalty improvement initiatives

---

## Key Business Insights
- Month-to-month contracts exhibit the highest churn risk
- Long-term customers contribute significantly higher revenue
- Electronic check users demonstrate elevated churn behavior
- Customers with shorter tenure are more likely to churn
- High monthly charge customers generate stronger revenue performance

---

## Strategic Recommendations
- Improve retention programs for short-term customers
- Promote long-term contracts through incentives
- Enhance onboarding experience for new customers
- Optimize payment experience and customer support
- Strengthen loyalty programs for high-value customers
